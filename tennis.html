US Open 2021

On Sep 3, 2021, I was very blessed and thankful for having the opportunity to watch the US Open at Flushing Meadows, Queens, NY. My brother and I were able to get tickets to watch the Men's and Women's 3rd Round match that day, and it was both our first time to watch the game in person. Being tennis fans and players of the sport, we had fun watching the games and seeing the players in person.
