Classical Architecture

On January 2, 2012 I took an interim program for Classical Architecture and Design at the Institute of Classical Architecture & Arts (ICAA )at New York City. In this program I learned how to draw, identify, render, and compose classical elements to form designs of buildings that follow classical proportions and traditions.
