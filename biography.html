Personal Biography

Paul Estipona is a junior undergraduate student at Florida State University (FSU) majoring in Computer Science. He is passionate about software development, particularly developing websites, and front-end applications.

Paul has a diverse set of skills, his background includes serving in the Army for 6 years as a UH-60 Black Hawk Maintainer at Fort Carson, CO and later as a Cyber Operations Specialist at the National Security Agency (NSA) at Fort Meade, MD. Paul has a distinguished career in the military earning 2 Army Good Conduct Medals, 2 Army Commendation Medals, an Army Achievement Medal, Global War on Terror Service Medal, and a National Defense Service Medal. Paul was also an Honor Graduate at the US Navy’s Joint Cyber Analysis Course, graduating as one of the top students of his class, and he is also the Distinguished Graduate for the US Army’s Aviation Logistics School.

After transitioning from the military, Paul worked as a Cyber Security Watch Officer for Leidos at the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) securing Department of Defense (DoD) Networks as well as being a first responder for cyber related events. Paul also worked for Amazon Web Services (AWS) as a Cloud Support Engineer Level 3 maintaining AWS’s CloudFormation services. Paul now works as a Software Systems Engineer for Leidos developing analytical tools for the DoD.

In his spare time Paul loves to watch historical documentaries, study math, draw classical architectural drawings, and be a full-time Dad. Paul also stays active by traveling, playing tennis and ride his mountain bike on local trails in the DC, MD, and VA area.
